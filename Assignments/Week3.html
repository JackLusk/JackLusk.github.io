Week 3

In the Laser Cutting process, I start by creating a design using the templates provided on the class website page. Once the template I chose is saved as a .svg, in my case it is a Curved Box design, I can import it into the Laser Cutter to begin the engraving and cutting process.


Then, I imported the design into Adobe Illustrator, and then made edits so that it fit my standards.


Lastly, I import the design into the Laser Cutter, and after turning on the vacuum and machine, begin the cut.


After the cutting and folding into the box shape, it's done! Very usable for storage of small items.
