Vinyl Cutting is a form of digital fabrication, as it utilized programs to create the design that the cutter will slice, and then proceeds to relay that design to the cutter. Without a digital aspect of Vinyl Cutting, designs would have to be made freehand or with mechanical means, adding much more intense and unnecesarry labor to the project.


Raster graphics are made by adjusting the brightness coloration of pixels to create an image, while vector graphics use mathmatically derived shapes to create images. When used in a low pixel count, raster graphics can have poor definition, while vector graphics retain their definition.
